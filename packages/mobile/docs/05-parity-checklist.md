# Full parity checklist

## Viewport
- Pan, pinch zoom, and double tap zoom (if used on web).
- Smooth animated focus on node or selection.

## Nodes
- Tap select, multi-select (if used on web).
- Drag nodes with snapping or alignment behavior.
- Context actions (delete, duplicate, inspect).

## Edges
- Port-to-port creation with live preview.
- Edge selection and deletion.
- Visual parity for stroke, color, and selection state.

## Interaction
- No gesture conflicts that block core editing.
- Taps and drags are reliable at small hit targets.

## Stability
- Works on both iOS and Android.
- Acceptable performance at current graph size.
